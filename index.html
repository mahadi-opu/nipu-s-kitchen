About Me

Hi, I’m Syed Mohaiminul Habib Nipu. I’m the chef and owner of Nipu's Kitchen. I’ve been a personal chef for going on 2 years. I received my training as a Personal Chef through the Bangladesh Department of Youth Development and also have a diploma in Indian Food and Chines Food from PCDI.

My Recipies

No matter what I’m cooking, my primary goal is to create a tasty meal. I am trained in Indian and Chinese style and specialize in South Indian Mexican. Additionally, I have experience with Asian and Mediterranean cooking styles

Mutton Curry with Potatoes

This mutton curry is very traditional in every home of Bangladesh. This village-style recipe consists of very common spices avoiding use of a lot of spices for easy and fast cooking.

Homestyle Chicken Korma

I love my Kormas. Most of all I love Bangladeshi Kormas, which tend to be lightly spiced but heavily flavored. We do not put a lot of heavy cream like the Indian version but prefer onions and ghee to give the creaminess

Chicken Pakora

Okay so this isn’t a traditional Bangladeshi dish, but given our love for everything deep fried, it is a welcome addition to my table anytime, at the dinner table or as a snack.
